Calorie Rush: The Marathon

Introduction

Game Concept

Welcome to "Calorie Rush: The Marathon" – the ultimate endless runner game where every step burns calories and every obstacle is a chance to test your fitness limits! Get ready to lace up your running shoes and dive into a non-stop adventure through bustling city streets, serene countryside, and challenging terrains. In "Calorie Rush: The Marathon," the road stretches endlessly before you, filled with obstacles and NPCs vying for the top spot. Your goal? To run as far as you can, dodging obstacles and burning calories with every stride. It's not just about reaching the finish line – it's about pushing your limits and achieving your personal best. As you dash through the ever-changing landscape, obstacles will come at you fast and furious. From towering barricades to tricky traps, each hurdle tests your reflexes and determination. Stay focused, stay sharp, and keep moving forward to rack up those calorie-burning miles. But fear not, for you have an array of power-ups and items at your disposal to help you along the way. Spend your hard-earned coins wisely to purchase second chances, giving you a lifeline when you need it most. Need an extra burst of speed? Activate the speed buff to leave your competitors in the dust. And for those truly challenging moments, the invincible buff will make you unstoppable, allowing you to plow through obstacles with ease. But remember, in the world of "Calorie Rush: The Marathon," every step counts and every calorie burned brings you closer to victory. It's not just a race – it's a journey of self-improvement and fitness achievement. So lace up your shoes, hit the pavement, and embark on an endless adventure of fitness and fun in "Calorie Rush: The Marathon!" How far can you run?

Key Achievement

Calorie Rush: The Marathon marks as my very first Unity Project. Game controls, animation controller, soundeffects and world building point out the key achievements acquired while developing this game. I implemented a pooling system that allowed me to build an endless world cost efficiently. The use of power ups not only helps the player to get further in game but also adds to its fun game mechanics.
